About the Dataset

Dataset

You can find the source of the dataset here.

Dataset consists of two columns: Comment and Label. This dataset contains 18,581 comments, and Label is labeled according to the class of cyberbullying: Age, Ethnicity, Gender, Religion, Other types of cyberbullying, and Not cyberbullying.

The data has been balanced to contain approximately 11,000 comments of label:1 and 7,000 comments of label:0. Preprocessed the data from the Youtube comments, these Comments either describe a Cyberbullying event or Not-Cyberbullying.

Dataset Sample

Cyberbullying Type Classes Description

  • Age: This class refers to instances of cyberbullying that revolve around someone's age. It could involve making fun of someone’s age or using age-related insults to hurt them.
  • Ethnicity: This class refers to tweets that involve bad words targeted at someone's ethnicity.
  • Gender: Involves attacking someone based on their gender.
  • Religion: Cyberbullying that is targeted at a person's religion or beliefs.
  • Other Types of Cyberbullying: This class covers any form of online harassment that doesn’t fit the defined categories but still causes harm and distress.
  • Not Cyberbullying: Situations where the content or behavior does not meet the criteria for cyberbullying.
